Freedom and Corona

Due to the current restrictions caused by the Corona virus, many discussions about "freedom" have been started. How much freedom do I have to give away? Is it a violation of personal freedom or the freedom of others if I follow or break certain rules? A **balance between individual freedom and the freedom of the collective** follows. Based on this topic, a game-like application will be developed, which resembles a kind of simulation on the one hand and a puzzle principle on the other. The game will be implemented with **p5 and the p5.play library**.
